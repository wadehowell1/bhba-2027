PAYMENT OF FINES — Mandatory Rules
1
ALL FINES MUST BE PAID PRIOR TO AN OFFENDING PLAYER / TEAM / OFFICIAL'S NEXT GAME.
2
THE TEAM / PLAYER / COACH / TEAM OFFICIAL WILL NOT BE ALLOWED TO PARTICIPATE IN ANY GAME UNTIL ALL OUTSTANDING FINES ARE PAID. — No Pay, No Play policy is absolute.
3
Payment must be made by verified wire transfer or manager's cheque delivered to the BHBA Administration Office. Cash payments are not accepted.
Disclaimer: The contents of this document are not exhaustive. Any other offence(s) or penalty(ies) not stated herein which are deemed as bringing the operations of the BHBA into disrepute will be dealt with at the discretion of the BHBA. All monetary amounts are in Jamaican Dollars (JMD). This schedule is reviewed and updated annually by the BHBA Executive Committee.
