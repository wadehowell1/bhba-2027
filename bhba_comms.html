📖 BHBA Template Authoring Guide

Every BHBA automated communication must be backed by a stored template.

Templates ensure consistency, auditability, and compliance across all BHBA communications — from fine notices to jersey clash alerts to registration confirmations.

Required Fields

  • Title — Unique, plain-language name. Used in dropdown lists.
  • Purpose — Why this template exists. Helps future admins understand its role.
  • Trigger — The system condition that activates this template. Be specific.
  • Header/Subject — Email subject line. Use [VARIABLE] placeholders.
  • Body — Full message body. Should include: greeting, context, required action, deadline if applicable.
  • Close — Signature block, legal disclaimer, or reply instruction.

Variable Reference

VariableReplaced With
[TEAM_NAME]Home / target team name
[OPPONENT_NAME]Opposing team name
[MATCH_DATE]Scheduled match date
[VENUE]Match venue / court
[AMOUNT]Fine or fee amount in JMD
[REF_NO]Transaction or reference number
[PLAYER_NAME]Player's full name
[EXEC_NAME]BHBA Executive issuing the notice
[DEADLINE]Payment or response deadline date
[SEASON]Competition season (e.g. 2026)

Template IDs

Use the format TPL-[CATEGORY]-[DESCRIPTOR] e.g. TPL-JERSEY-BIBS, TPL-FINANCE-FINE-NOTICE, TPL-REG-WELCOME.

Categories

  • jersey — Colour clash, bib requirements, reverse jersey notices
  • finance — Fine notices, payment reminders, receipts
  • registration — Welcome, approval, rejection, renewal
  • disciplinary — Suspension notices, conduct warnings, ejection confirmations
  • protest — Protest acknowledgement, hearing notice, ruling
  • general — Announcements, schedule updates, general notices
