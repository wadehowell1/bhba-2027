📋 Protest & Fine Workflow

1. Fine Levied — BHBA Executive attaches fine to team/player. DR: Team Account | CR: BHBA Fine Revenue.

2. Payment Window — Team has 14 days to settle. After 14 days, No Pay No Play rule enforced.

3. Payment Confirmed — Wire transfer or manager's cheque cleared. DR: Cash/Bank | CR: Team Receivable.

4. Protest Lodged — Team may file protest within 7 days of payment. BHBA logs protest, schedules hearing.

5. Hearing — Match Commissioner or BHBA Executive panel reviews. Ruling: Upheld (fine stands) or Overturned (fine reversed).

6. Fine Overturned — Contra entry posted: DR: BHBA Fine Revenue | CR: Cash (refund). Accounts balanced. Net effect = zero.

7. Fine Upheld — No refund. Fine income remains on BHBA statement.

⚖️ Levy Fine or Fee
Only authorised BHBA Executives may levy fines and fees. All entries are permanent and auditable.
